Hacking



What is Hacking ?

Hacking refers to activities that seek to compromise digital devices, such as computers, smartphones, tablets, and even entire networks.The term also may refer to anyone who uses their abilities to gain unauthorized access to systems or networks in order to commit crimes.


Different Types of Hacking

  • White Hat / Ethical Hackers
  • Black Hat Hackers.
  • Gray Hat Hackers.
  • Script Kiddies.
  • Green Hat Hackers.
  • Blue Hat Hackers
  • Red Hat Hackers
  • State/Nation Sponsored Hackers.

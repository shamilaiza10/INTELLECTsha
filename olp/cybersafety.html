Cybersecurity and Tips for Safe Browsing



Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These cyberattacks are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information; extorting money from users via ransomware; or interrupting normal business processes


Tips for Safe Internet Browsing

  • update your browser frequently
  • turn two-factor authentication whenever possible
  • customize security settings
  • confirm site security (https or http)
  • use reputable shopping site
  • backup your data
  • avoid clicking on links if possible from message or chats
  • bookmark important sites

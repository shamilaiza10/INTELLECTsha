Cyberbullying



cyberbullying is the use of the internet and related technologies to harm other people in a deliberate , repeated , and hostile manner .


Different Forms of Cyberbullying

  • Flaming

    • Online fights using electronic messages with angry and vulgar language are referred to as Flaming.

  • Harassment

    • Repeatedly sending nasty , mean and insultingmessages is called Harassment.

  • Denigration

    • "Dissing" someone online ; sending or posting gossip or rumors about a person to damage his or her reputation or friendship is known as Denigration.

  • Impersonation

    • pretending to be someone else and sending or posting materials to get that person in trouble or in danger , or to damage that person's friendship or reputation is known as Impersonation.

  • Outing

    • sharing someone's secrets or embarrassing information or images online is called Outing

  • Trickery

    • talking to someone into revealing secrets or embarrassing information , and then share it in online is reffered to as Trickery.

  • Exclusion

    • intentionally and cruelly excluding someone from an online group is called Exclusion.

  • Cyberstalking

    • repeated intense harassment and denigration , that include threats or create significant fear is known as Cyberstalking
